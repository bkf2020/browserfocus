How whitelist blocking works

Note: Please type rules in the format website.com. Do NOT include www or / at the end!

Examples:

  • Typing example.com WILL WHITELIST example.com AND a.example.com AND example.com/test
  • Typing a.example.com WILL WHITELIST a.example.com but NOT example.com. It WILL whitelist b.a.example.com
  • Typing example.com/test WILL whitelist example.com/test AND example.com/test/test AND a.example.com/test BUT NOT example.com.
